# Code of Conduct

Manga CMS uses a simple public contribution standard:

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent, but respond to impact when something causes harm.
- Keep feedback focused on the work, not the person.
- Do not harass, threaten, dox, or target contributors.
- Do not post private manuscripts, credentials, personal data, or production
  operational details.

Maintainers may edit, hide, close, or remove issues, pull requests, comments, or
other contributions that are abusive, unsafe, spammy, or outside the public OSS
scope of this repository.

For security-sensitive reports, do not open a public issue. Follow
[SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md).
